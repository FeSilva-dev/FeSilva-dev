## What I Build (professionally)

> Most of my work lives in private repos — here's what I ship daily.

**🔋 77Sol** — Brazil's largest solar energy platform
- Systems processing hundreds of financing proposals/day
- Blog with SSR/SSG driving organic traffic via SEO
- High-conversion landing pages (days → hours to publish)
- Energy savings calculator boosting pre-sale engagement
- Test suites with Jest, Cypress & Playwright

**📺 FGG Company** — Samsung Smart TV streaming app
- React + Tizen SDK native build for television
- Remote control navigation, custom HTML5 video player
- TV authentication via remote or dashboard-generated code
- Multi-profile dashboard with Kids mode & content control

**✈️ Touchway** — Airport vehicle monitoring @ Viracopos (largest cargo airport in LATAM)
- Real-time vehicle tracking on interactive airport maps
- Geofenced infraction zones with map-drawn boundaries
- React Native inspection app with photo upload & digital signature
- +30% operational efficiency

**🌾 Senar-MT** — Mobile apps for rural producers
- Offline-first architecture with local DB & pending sync queue
- Real-time GPS tracking for route auditing & driver payments
- Forum system, weather forecasts, agricultural indicators

**🛒 Via (Casas Bahia)** — One of Brazil's largest e-commerce platforms
- Post-sale workflows (returns & exchanges) for millions of users
- Frontend fraud prevention reducing chargebacks
